Our History

In 2009, our culinary journey began with a dream and a commitment to redefine dining experiences. Founded by Chef Gabrielle Laurent, Table.com started as a small bistro, drawing inspiration from both classical French techniques and global flavors.

As the years unfolded, Table.com transformed into a culinary haven, earning accolades for its innovative approach. In 2017, the restaurant received its first Michelin star, a testament to Chef Laurent's dedication to pushing culinary boundaries.

The following decade marked a period of expansion and evolution. Chef Laurent's protégé, Chef Alex Reynolds, infused modern techniques while preserving the restaurant's core values. This era saw the introduction of signature dishes and a commitment to sustainable sourcing.

Today, Table.com stands as a beacon of gastronomic excellence. Our history is a tapestry of passion, creativity, and a relentless pursuit of culinary perfection. Join us as we continue to craft memorable dining experiences and embark on a journey that celebrates the artistry of food.
